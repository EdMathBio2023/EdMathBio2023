Hi Edinburgh Mathematical Biology Conference

Separation of Timescales in Biology

24-25 May 2023

About EdMathBio Conference

The conference will be a two-day event, on Wednesday 24th and Thursday 25th May, consisting of 14 plenary talks and a poster session.

The conference will be held in person, at the Bayes centre (room G.03) of the University of Edinburgh.

For any information please email Tatiana Filatova at tfilatov@ed.ac.uk

Registration

This conference happens in person and there are limited free spaces for attendees and posters.

If you register to present a poster your attendance registration will happen automatically.

You need to complete a separate registration form if you would like to attend the Conference Dinner (see below).

To attend this event please register here.

To present a poster at this event please instead register here. Please note that the poster session is on Wednesday, May 24th at 17:30-19:30.

If you would like to attend the conference dinner please also register here.
